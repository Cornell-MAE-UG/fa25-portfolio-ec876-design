## Overview
The homework assignment that I would be is homework 1. Homework 1 not only introduced but also strengthened my understanding of heat transfer, in particular, the way to correctly model heat transfer problems. This assignment required me to distinguish between different types of mode of heat transfer: radiation, convection, and conduction in different environment,reinforcing the fact that the governing mode only depended on the physical situation that the problem is involved in. The most valuable part of the homework showed me that you can combine different types of heat transfer into one problem, such as in the homework where both convection and radiation was used. Different techniques such as applying Fourier's law in 1D steady state conduction demonstrated how temperature scale with material properties and other factors such as thickness.
